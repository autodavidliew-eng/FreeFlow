# SLA and Support Model

This document defines support tiers and response expectations for FreeFlow.

## Support Tiers

- **Standard:** Business hours support
- **Premium:** 24/7 support with faster response

## Response Times (Example)

- **Sev-1:** 1 hour (Premium), 4 hours (Standard)
- **Sev-2:** 4 hours (Premium), 1 business day (Standard)
- **Sev-3:** 1 business day (Premium), 3 business days (Standard)

## Escalation Path

1. Support engineer triages and acknowledges ticket.
2. On-call engineer engaged for Sev-1/Sev-2.
3. Engineering manager and product owner notified for Sev-1.

## Notes

- Define business hours and holiday coverage.
- Review SLA quarterly.
